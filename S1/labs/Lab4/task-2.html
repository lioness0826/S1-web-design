Detailed Recipe for Stir-Fried Pork with Okra

Online shopping  More information

The two main technical challenges of the recipe: removing the pork's gamey smell and managing the moisture of the Okra.

The steps are as follows

  • Add minced ginger to the sliced and drained pork, and marinate for 10-15 minutes.
  • Wash the okra, drain the water, and cut it into pieces.
  • Prepare two cloves of garlic and chop them, then prepare an appropriate amount of fermented black beans and chili bean paste.
  • Add some oil to the pan, and after it heats up, add the pork. Cook over medium heat until all the moisture in the pan evaporates.
  • Add the remaining seasonings and the okra, and stir-fry until the okra is cooked.
